Eric Foultz

Eric Foultz is a Senior Managing Director at LBJ Family Wealth Advisors in Austin, Texas.  He serves on the development board of the Nature Conservancy and the board of trustees for the Austin Museum of Art  He is also on the Advisory Council for the Department of Accounting at Texas A&M University and is a member of the San Antonio Charro Association.  Eric received Bachelor of Business Administration and Master of Science degrees from Texas A&M University.

Eric is married to Alice Carrington Foultz and they have four children.  Eric is a most fortunate Texan to live in both Austin and San Antonio.
