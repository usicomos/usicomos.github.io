USICOMOS 15th ANNUAL INTERNATIONAL SCIENTIFIC SYMPOSIUM

Registration

 

The registration fee includes three receptions (Thursday, Friday, and Saturday evenings) and the field tours to the Missions (Thursday afternoon) and the Alamo (Saturday morning). The Modern Movement pre-conference tour (Wednesday) is offered at an additional cost (see registration form)

 

The El Tropicano Hotel is the conference hotel for the US/ICOMOS Symposium. All plenary sessions will be held at the hotel.

 

The pre-conference Modern Movement tour will leave from and return to the hotel.

 

The Thursday afternoon field tour of the Missions will leave from the hotel.  Most participants will conclude the field tour at Mission San Jose for the welcome reception. A separate bus will leave from the hotel for Mission San Jose for those who did not take the afternoon field tour.

 

The Friday evening reception at the Southwest Art Center is within walking distance from the hotel.

 

Travel to the Saturday evening closing reception will be by barge up the San Antonio River and through the locks. Attendees may arrange for their own transportation at the conclusion of the reception or return by taking a scenic walk down the Riverwalk to the hotel or your own dinner reservation.
