Pamela Jary Rosser

Pamela Jary Rosser PA AIC is native to San Antonio. She grew up in the art and architecture world. She graduated with a degree in fine art and a minor in art history from the University of Incarnate Word. She studied art history in Italy and is a Professional Associate of the American Institute for Conservation.

Pamela has been a conservator in private practice with her business partner/mother, Mary Canales “Cisi” Jary for over twenty years. Together their company, Restoration Associates Limited excelled in preserving historic projects including the Moody Mansion, Empire Theatre, Mission Concepcion, The Alamo and many more.

In 2009 Pamela left Restoration Associates Limited to be the first onsite conservator at the Alamo. www.thealamo.org

Pamela has been married for twenty years and has two lovely daughters.
