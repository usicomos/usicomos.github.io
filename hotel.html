USICOMOS 15th ANNUAL INTERNATIONAL SCIENTIFIC SYMPOSIUM

The El Tropicano Riverwalk will serve as the conference hotel for the symposium. Therefore, all plenary sessions will be held in the hotel’s meeting facilities and all field tours will originate at the hotel.

El Tropicano Riverwalk is a “resort style” tropical-themed hotel that has 306 well-appointed guest rooms and 37,000 square feet of meeting space. As a full-service convention hotel, El Tropicano Riverwalk also offers a pool, sundeck, fitness facility, and a lounge that overlooks the San Antonio River. The hotel houses a restaurant and the “Expreso” Grab-and-Go coffee bar.

The hotel is offering a rate of $85.00 per night (single or double occupancy) plus tax. The conference rate is available up to 3 days prior and 3 days following the dates of the conference.

When calling the reservation office, please identify yourself as being with US/ICOMOS to obtain the conference rate.

Please call the reservation desk 1-877-214-9768 or email at  reservations@eltropicanohotel.com.

The Hotel will hold the US/ICOMOS rate of $85.00 per night (occupancy tax not included) until April 30th, 2012.

Any individual cancellations received less than 72 hours prior to arrival will be billed for one night plus tax.
